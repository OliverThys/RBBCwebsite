Download Yuzu Emulator for Windows

Yuzu is the best Nintendo Switch Emulator for Windows and the most stable one available. This is the official final release of version 1734 released earlier this year. Simply download Yuzu on your Windows PC and double-click on the .exe file to launch the installer. Detailed installation instructions can be found here.

Allows you to emulate any Switch game on platforms including Android, Windows and Linux.

  • Resolution scaling allows you to increase the visual display of a game and make them look more crisp and clear.
  • Multiplayer support for playing online.
  • If you want to preserve your game status, you can do so via exporting your game saves.
  • Supports Vulkan and OpenGL API.
  • Modding support.
  • Compatible with over 100+ games.
  • Unofficially works on Steam Deck.
  • Allows you to play with Xbox and PlayStation controllers.
  • Lightweight and portable.

Note: Yuzu Emulator cannot be downloaded due to legal implications that the authors had and the files are not available anywhere.
